Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy TSHIDI'S KITCHEN products?

Our products are available at select stores across Limpopo and through our direct delivery service. Check our Products page for more information.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15km radius of our kitchen. For larger orders or catering, we can arrange delivery to wider areas.

Are your products suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

Many of our products are vegetarian-friendly. We're working on expanding our range to accommodate various dietary needs. Please contact us with specific requirements.

Can I visit your kitchen?

Yes! We welcome visitors during our operating hours. You can sample our products and see where the magic happens.

Do you ship products internationally?

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. We're exploring international shipping options for the future.
